Online Food Delivery System (OFDS) is used by many companies for delivering food at the doorstep. Each company has a name. In addition, companies hire employees for food delivery. The type of an employee can be a part-time employees or a full-time employees. Each employee has a name. The system also allows the company to manage multiple vehicles. Each vehicle has a registration number. Also, the type of a vehicle can be an owned vehicle or a rented vehicle. An employee may be asigned to a vehicle as a driver for food delivery.
